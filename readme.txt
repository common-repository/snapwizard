Embed Your Instagram Feed in your WordPress website in a Snap with SnapWizard. No coding skills required!

== Description ==

Embedding your Instagram feed into your WordPress website with SnapWizard offers a transformative and effortlessly seamless experience, enhancing the visual appeal and interactivity of your online presence. SnapWizard not only eliminates the complexities of coding but also ensures a dynamic, real-time showcase of your latest Instagram content on your website. The user-friendly process allows even those with minimal technical expertise to effortlessly blend their curated web content with the vibrant tapestry of their social media, creating a unified and cohesive brand narrative. By erasing the boundaries between platforms, SnapWizard fosters a deeper connection with your audience, providing them with a holistic experience that extends beyond a static website, leaving a lasting and engaging impression. So, why settle for a conventional website when you can elevate it into a dynamic, visually compelling journey with your Instagram feed seamlessly embedded in just a snap?

== SnapWizard Features ==

- No coding skills required!
- Associate a long-lived access token in a snap!
- Decide which type of Instagram content you want to embed: images, video, carousel, or all of them!
- Associate a WordPress author to the embed post and media file.
- Associate one or more WordPress categories to the embed post and media file.
- Decide how many API calls can be made by setting a limit, avoiding exceeding the limit imposed by Meta.
- Exclude one or more elements of the feed.
- Easy Feed Processor Endpoint to integrate to your cron job
- Stats dashboard

== Installation ==

1. Upload the ZIP file through the 'Plugins > Add New > Upload' screen in your WordPress dashboard.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. For further information, read the DOC.md included within the plugin
